ABOUT ME
Catherine Wilson is a senior at the Philip Merrill College of Journalism in College Park, MD completing her undergraduate degree in multiplatform journalism with an emphasis in print journalism. Wilson is set to graduate in Fall 2021.

While finishing her degree, Wilson spends her time working as a staff writer for the Southern Maryland Chronicle where she writes investigative features, enterprise stories, and hard news stories on a variety of topics including business, local government, and politics centered in the Southern Maryland community.

In addition to writing, Wilson has also spent time working as a member of the copy editing team at the Diamondback editing articles for publication.
